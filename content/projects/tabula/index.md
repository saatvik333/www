tabula is a minimal new tab extension for browsers that replaces your default new tab page with a clean, distraction-free interface.

## features

- clean, minimal design
- quick links to your favorite sites
- weather widget
- quote of the day
- customizable background

## installation

available on the chrome web store and firefox add-ons.
